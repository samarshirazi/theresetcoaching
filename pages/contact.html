Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about coaching with Flora

What happens during a free consultation?

During our 45-minute consultation, we'll discuss your current situation, goals, and challenges. I'll share how coaching could help you and determine if we're a good fit to work together. There's no pressure or sales pitch—just a genuine conversation about your growth and success.

How long does coaching typically last?

Most clients work with me for 3-6 months to achieve significant transformation. However, the length depends on your goals and situation. Some clients see results in just a few sessions, while others prefer ongoing coaching for continued growth and accountability.

Do you offer coaching sessions via video call?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide through secure video conferencing. Many clients actually prefer this format as it eliminates travel time and allows for more flexible scheduling. The coaching experience is just as effective as in-person sessions.

What if I'm not sure which type of coaching I need?

That's exactly what our free consultation is for! We'll explore your situation together and I'll recommend the best approach. Many clients benefit from a combination of business and personal development coaching, as they often go hand in hand.

What's your coaching philosophy?

I believe in personalized, science-based coaching that addresses both mindset and action. Just like athletes need coaches to reach peak performance, professionals need guidance to unlock their potential. My approach combines practical strategies with psychological insights to create lasting transformation.
